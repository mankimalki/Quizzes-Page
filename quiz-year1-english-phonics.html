Year 1 Phonics Practice

Welcome to our interactive phonics quizzes! These activities are designed to help Year 1 students master essential phonics skills including letter sounds, blending, segmenting, and common phonetic patterns. Each quiz includes audio pronunciation to support learning.

Click the speaker icon to hear the correct pronunciation before taking each quiz.

Interactive Practice

Repetition

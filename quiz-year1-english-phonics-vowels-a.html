Short 'a' Sound Practice

Welcome to our interactive short 'a' sound quiz! The short 'a' sound (/æ/) is one of the most common vowel sounds in English. You'll hear this sound in many everyday words.

a

The Short 'a' Sound

The short 'a' sound is represented by the IPA symbol /æ/. It's the sound you hear in words like:

  • cat /k/ /æ/ /t/
  • bat /b/ /æ/ /t/
  • hat /h/ /æ/ /t/

Learning Tips

Practice Daily: Spend 5-10 minutes each day practicing the short 'a' sound.
Listen Carefully: Pay attention to how your mouth moves when making the /æ/ sound.
Read Aloud: Practice reading simple books that contain many short 'a' words.

Complete 11 Plus ISEB Subject Coverage

Our quizzes cover all essential subjects tested by ISEB, ensuring students are well-prepared for their 11 Plus exams:

English

The 11 Plus English section tests reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and writing skills. Our quizzes cover Reading Comprehension, Grammar (parts of speech, sentence structure), Vocabulary (word meanings, synonyms, antonyms), Writing (composition skills), Spelling, Punctuation, Cloze Passages, Inference, Figurative Language (metaphors, similes), and Literary Devices. These quizzes help students develop the literacy skills crucial for success.

Mathematics

The 11 Plus Maths section tests core mathematical concepts and problem-solving abilities. Our quizzes cover Addition and Subtraction, Multiplication and Division, Fractions, Decimals and Percentages, Measurement, Geometry, Properties of Shapes, Ratio and Proportion, Statistics, Number Patterns and Sequences, Money Problems, Time, Simple Algebra, Order of Operations, Coordinates, and Volume. These quizzes ensure students are confident with the mathematical skills needed for the exam.

Verbal Reasoning

Verbal Reasoning assesses a student's ability to understand and reason using words. Our quizzes cover essential topics like Alphabet Sequences, Word Codes, Odd One Out, Anagrams, Word Relationships, Compound Words, Opposites, Synonyms, Letter Series, Cloze Passages, Double Letters, Hidden Words, Missing Links, Related Words, Letter Connections, and Combining Words. These quizzes help students develop the vocabulary and logical thinking skills crucial for success.

Non-Verbal Reasoning

Non-Verbal Reasoning tests visual problem-solving skills using shapes, diagrams, and pictures. Our quizzes include Series, Odd One Out, Analogies, Grouping, Pattern Completion, Rotation, Reflection, Matrices, 3D Shapes, Nets, Dice and Cubes, Codes, Layering, and Folding. These interactive exercises help students master the visual-spatial reasoning required for the 11+.

How to Use These Quizzes for Effective 11 Plus ISEB Preparation

  1. Start Early: Begin using the quizzes several months before the 11+ exams to identify areas needing focus.
  2. Mix It Up: Alternate between different subjects and topics to keep practice engaging.
  3. Focus on Weaknesses: Use quiz results to target specific areas for more revision.
  4. Simulate Test Conditions: Occasionally time quizzes to get used to working within exam constraints.
  5. Review and Learn: Always go over answers, especially incorrect ones, to understand the concepts better.

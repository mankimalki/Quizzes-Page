Short Vowel Sounds Practice

Welcome to our interactive vowel sounds quiz! This activity will help you practice the five short vowel sounds in English: a, e, i, o, u. Each vowel has its own unique sound that you'll hear in many words.

Click the speaker icon to hear the correct pronunciation before taking each quiz.

Learning Objectives

Recognize and produce the short vowel sounds: a, e, i, o, u
Distinguish between different vowel sounds when listening
Identify vowel sounds in simple CVC (consonant-vowel-consonant) words
